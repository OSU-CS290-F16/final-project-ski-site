Mount Bachelor

Mount Bachelor stands at 9,068 ft and is positioned near central Oregon in the Cascade Mountain range. It has an average snowfall of 460 inches per year! Thats around the same as Mount Hood Meadows while being a fraction of the size! Sitting only 20 minutes outside bend, many visitors take lodge there while, during the day, going to the mountain for a action-packed skiing vacation. Mount Bachelor ski area is its only notable ski resort, however, Bachelor still is Oregons largest skiing site.

Mount Bachelors ski resorts:

  • Mount Bachelor skiing

Mount Bachelor skiing offers both ski and snowboard options for the whole family! As well as tubing, sled dog rides and snowshoe Tours. Even someone who is not into skiing will have something fun to do for the whole weekend! Plan your trip quick as passes are going fast!
